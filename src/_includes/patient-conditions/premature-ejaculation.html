Premature ejaculation (PE) is a male sexual dysfunction characterized by ejaculation which almost always occurs within approximately one minute of vaginal penetration and this results in distress for the male. There are 3 common themes in patients with premature ejaculations:

1

They have a rapid ejaculation usually less than one minute (Realize that the average ejaculatory time in most men in 5.4 minutes).

2

These men feel that they have a lack of self-control about their timing of ejaculation.

3

They experience distress and interpersonal difficulty which can also lead to frustration and avoidance of sexual intimacy.
